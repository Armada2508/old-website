Why donate to Stillwater Armada Robotics? Support future leaders.

Although this robotics team has several amazing coaches and mentors, the majority of Armada's functions are student-led. Many of our members will move on to universities and careers that will utilize the leadership skills learned right here in Armada Robotics.

Foster innovation and creativity.

Here at Armada, we use knowledge and experimentation in woodworking, metalworking, software developement, networking, electronics, wiring, and telecommunications, to achieve an objective. This year's young robot-builders may be our world's future problem-solvers. Please, keep the flame of STEM alive by contributing to a robotics team's efforts.

Encourage collaboration.

Armada upholds a strong sense of cooporation between the different small groups of people that focus on design, mechanics, woodworking, metalworking, electronics, and programming. Armada would be unsuccessful without collaboration, just as many of the world's greatest organizations.
